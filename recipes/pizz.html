Spicy Italian Pizza

This homemade Spicy Italian Pizza is pretty darn amazing if I do say so myself.This spicy Italian pizza has some of the same fillings that I love in my favorite Subway sandwich along with some other standard pizza toppings!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Italian Sausage
  • Deli Ham
  • Pepperoni
  • Hard Salami
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Directions

  1. Place your crust on your baking sheet or a baking stone.
  2. Top your dough with your favorite pizza sauce or some gussied up on tomato sauce then begin layering on your toppings!
  3. Bake the pizza for about 15 minutes at 450-degrees
  4. To add a little more spice, top your pizza with red pepper flakes once it comes out of the oven!
